Project Initiation & Bethlehem Involvement

Project Overview

The Project Initiation & Bethlehem Involvement initiative focuses on establishing strong partnerships with the City of Bethlehem and surrounding communities to identify, develop, and implement engineering solutions for local challenges.

Mission

To create meaningful collaborations with Bethlehem community organizations, government agencies, and residents to address infrastructure and development needs through engineering expertise and student engagement.

Key Activities

  • Community outreach and needs assessment
  • Partnership development with city officials and local organizations
  • Project scoping and feasibility studies
  • Stakeholder engagement and communication
  • Resource mobilization and project planning

Current Partnerships

  • City of Bethlehem Engineering Department
  • Bethlehem Area School District
  • Local environmental organizations
  • Community development groups
  • Neighborhood associations

Project Development Process

  1. Community engagement and needs identification
  2. Technical feasibility assessment
  3. Stakeholder consultation and feedback
  4. Project design and planning
  5. Implementation and monitoring

Impact

This initiative serves as the foundation for all local EWB projects, ensuring that our work is community-driven, technically sound, and aligned with local priorities and needs.

Get Involved

Students interested in community engagement, project management, and public service are encouraged to join this team. Gain experience in stakeholder management, project development, and community relations.
